Style Guidelines

Create Heirarchy
Important elements must be the most demanding of the user's attention. Prioritize your list and design accordingly using size, color, weight and placement.
Provide Focus
Be intentional about what you'd like the user to do. Promote only one action and make it front-and-center.
Eye Line
Keep viewers’ tendencies in mind when designing. Top to bottom, left to right eventually morphs into Hold my hand, I’ll show you where to go.
Hide Complexity
Balance power with simplicity. Defer secondary features to hidden menus that can be accessed when you need it.
