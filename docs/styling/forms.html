The humble form

We've set out to create an easy and powerful form system. Minimal markup combined with our grid system makes layout fun and intuitive.

Forms need love too

Forms are everywhere. We like good forms, and we dislike bad forms even more. A lot goes into designing good forms and a bad form can ruin user experience. For tips and information on designing better forms we recommend Web Form Design and Defensive Design for the Web.
